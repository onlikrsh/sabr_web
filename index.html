How to Use

Simple Steps,
Stunning Results

1
Apply oil generously to the roots, scalp and along the length of your hair.
2
Keep oil overnight or for 4–5 hours to allow deep absorption into the follicles.
3
Wash out thoroughly with a gentle, minimal-lather shampoo.
4
Apply 3–4 times weekly for best and most visible results.
